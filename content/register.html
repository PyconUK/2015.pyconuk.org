Register

Use the form below to book your tickets. This year, you must order a ticket for the Saturday evening conference meal separately. Due to logistical problems encountered last year, we've limited the number of meal tickets to 150 place this year. It's a popular event, so book a place while you can.

To register your interest in attending as a sponsored Trans*Code participant please use this form and we'll be in touch.

To register as a Django Girls participant please fill in this form. Django Girls have helpfully explained how applications will be evaluated.

If you are applying to attend under the auspices of Trans*Code or DjangoGirls you do not have to buy a ticket.

For the unwaged, university students or those on a low income, please contact us directly for ways in which we can support your attendance. Such help, if offered, is entirely at the discretion of the PyCon UK organising committee.

(If you don't see a form above, please try with a different browser.)
