PyCon UK Returns!

PyCon UK, the community organised conference for the Python programming language in the United Kingdom, returns on Friday 16th - Monday 19th September 2016.

For the fifth year in a row, there will be an education track - we look forward to welcoming teaching colleagues and the young Pythonistas of tomorrow. We will also be joined by DjangoGirls and Trans*Code - both of whom will be providing Python training for new members of our community.

Following the success of 2015, we will be running another track for Python users in research and data science. This time we've called it a RSE (Research Software Engineer) Track in recognition of that fact that RSEs in the humanities and arts are active and welcome in our community.

If you'd like to contribute, volunteer or help in any other way with PyCon UK (which is completely organised by volunteers) then get in touch via the conference mailing list.
