Accelerate and scale your tests with nodepool

Yolanda Robla Mota

I will describe Nodepool, the Openstack Infra tool used to spin up virtual machines for testing, according to the realtime demand for tests.

I will explain how to setup it, and how to integrate with Gearman and Jenkins, to allow to run tests on real-time and scaling that automatically.

Link to project source code: https://github.com/yrobla/nodepool_job_launcher https://github.com/openstack-infra/nodepool

Additional OpenStack Infra documentation: http://docs.openstack.org/infra/system-config/nodepool.html
