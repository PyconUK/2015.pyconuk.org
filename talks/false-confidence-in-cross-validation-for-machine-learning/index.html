False confidence in cross-validation for machine learning

Calvin Giles

Cross-validation can be performed in many ways - KFold, Leave-out-out, bootstrapping, repeated etc.. Which one should you choose and will it give you an unbiased estimate of how your model will perform in the wild? I will answer these questions using a variety of simulated datasets that make and break different assumptions.
