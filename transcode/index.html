Trans*Code

Trans*Code is for trans* people and allies who want to draw attention to issues facing women and the transgender community while providing introductory programming workshops. Participants drawn from all skill levels and backgrounds are welcome.

The video above (created for ComputerWeekly) gives you a flavour of the sorts of things you can expect from the day.
