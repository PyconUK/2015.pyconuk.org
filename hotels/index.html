Hotels

Ibis

We have reserved 40 rooms for PyCon UK attendees at the Ibis Coventry Centre Hotel (located about 200 meters from the conference centre). Rooms are priced at £57 per night (compared to the regular £79 per night). Remember, we provide breakfast at the conference centre!

To take advantage of this deal we're afraid you're going to have to go "old school" and follow these instructions:

  • Download and fill in this PDF booking form.
  • The completed form needs to be sent directly to the hotel, quoting the reference number 917891, at the email address: h2793-am@accor.com.
  • Await confirmation from the hotel itself.

We understand that this is a sub-optimal solution. You'll just have to trust us that this is the path of least resistance.

If you encounter any problems with your booking please direct your enquiry at the hotel using the email address quoted above.
